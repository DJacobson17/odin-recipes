Rose's Luxury Pork Salad

Description

A garlicky pork dish with floral lychees, sweet coconut, sharp onions, and crispy salty accents in each bite.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 habanero chile, seeds removed
  • 13.5-oz. can coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon Champagne vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Kosher salt
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 12 oz. ground pork, preferably shoulder
  • 3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 cup basil
  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/3 cup salted, roasted peanuts
  • 3/4 teaspoon shichimi togarashi
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 20oz. can lychees, drained and halved
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Bring chile and coconut mil to a simmer in a large saucepan over medium heat and cool, stirring occasionally, until reduced by half, 10-15 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat; discard chile.
  3. Add vinegar and sugar, stirring until sugar dissolves; season with salt. Let cool
  4. Cook garlic and oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, stirring often, until garlic is golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  5. Using a slotted spoon, transfer garlic to paper towels to drain; season with salt
  6. Increase heat to medium-high. Add pork and red pepper flakes to skillet; season with salt and pepper.
  7. Cook, breaking up pork and pressing firmly to help brown, until cooked through and crisped in spots, 6-8 minutes.
  8. Spoon sauce into bowls and top with garlic, basil, cilantro, mint, paenuts, and togarashi.
  9. Spoon pork along with some pan drippings over; drizzle with lime juice.
  10. Top with lychees and onion.
