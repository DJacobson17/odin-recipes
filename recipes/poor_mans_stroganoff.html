Poor Man's Stroganoff

Description

A traditional savory beef and noodle dish reimagined with ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning (such as Cavender's)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 pint sour cream

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lighlty salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir in beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; frain and discard grease. Season beef with Greek seasoning, salt and pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a separate skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir mushrooms in hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes; add to the ground beef.
  4. Stir sour cream into ground beef mixture; cook and stir until hot, about 5 minutes. Serve over egg noodles.
